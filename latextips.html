公式对齐与换行


	       begin{equation}
	       begin{aligned}
	       R(S_2)&= p_2cdot S_2=sum_{iin mathcal{I}^+(p_2)}B_i+beta B_{l'}\
	       &leq sum_{iin mathcal{I}^+(p_2)}B_i+B_{l'}leq sum_{iin mathcal{I}^+(p_1)}B_i \
